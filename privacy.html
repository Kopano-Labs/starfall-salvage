Privacy Policy

Sovereign Identity

The game utilizes a device-bound Sovereign ID (a cryptographic identifier generated locally) to track pilot progression without extracting personal identifiers. Your biological identity selection (XY/XX) is stored locally to adapt the flight deck interface and is not used for tracking or profiling.

What we collect

The game stores local pilot metadata, including your callsign, high scores, run receipts, and optional squad affinity on your device (localStorage). If you explicitly sync with the Sovereign Portal, your callsign and scores are transmitted to Kopano Labs for 360 Ecosystem leaderboard integration.

What we do not collect

We do not require real names, email, or phone number to play. We do not sell personal data.

Offline play

Core gameplay and vault data work offline. Network is used only for optional leaderboard sync, chat, and app updates.

Children

Starfall Salvage is suitable for general audiences. Parents should supervise online features.
