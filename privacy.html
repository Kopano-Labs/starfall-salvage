Privacy Policy

Starfall Salvage by Kopano Labs. Last updated: 14 May 2026.

What we collect

The game stores a local pilot callsign, scores, run receipts, and optional squad code on your device (localStorage and IndexedDB). If you sign in or sync, callsign and scores may be sent to Kopano Labs servers for leaderboard and community features.

What we do not collect

We do not require real names, email, or phone number to play. We do not sell personal data.

Offline play

Core gameplay and vault data work offline. Network is used only for optional leaderboard sync, chat, and app updates.

Children

Starfall Salvage is suitable for general audiences. Parents should supervise online features.
